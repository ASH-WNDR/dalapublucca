RUMUI

Rimui is an Atayal from Taoyuan City, Taiwan. She is a full-time comic artist who prefers professional stories. When she was in elementary school, she tried to be a voice actress for animation but was turned down because of her young age. She was then determined to become a comic artist by the time she'd be in high school. It took her ten more years to accomplish that dream.

She likes human-interest stories that describe everyday lives and is easily moved to tears. She wants to keep drawing comics, even if she remains a little-known artist. Her greatest aspiration is to create works that salvage the soul.

Funeral Concerto

In search of herself, Lin Chu-sheng quits university and ends up living and working for a funeral director. From her shock at seeing her first corpse, to becoming a practiced yet still compassionate employee, her experiences help her realize who she really is, and what she really wants.

Where are you going ?©Rimui Yumin /Yuan-Liou Publishing Co., Ltd.

Where are you going?

This comic combines thrills, supernatural elements, school life of teens, warm fuzzy feelings, and zany humor. It is the story of YU Qiu-dong, a loner in high school who suddenly starts seeing ghosts. After meeting Jing-jing, a classmate who also has the same sixth sense, and Uncle Feng-ji, a ghost with amnesia, Qui-dong goes through a series of strange yet emotionally moving experiences against a backdrop of life in Taiwan with touches of human warmth.
