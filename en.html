Chenghuang and I

Publisher:
Ever Glory Publishing Co., Ltd.
Author:
EBI

It is said that people have seven feelings and six desires. Interesting, isn't it? This year Chih Yao will be 28. Abandoned by her mother in the countryside when she was just a child, Chih Yao and her grandmother have depended on each other to survive. She never fell in love, and has never wanted to get married. But an unexpected event will turn her life upside down...When she opens her eyes again, Chih Yao discovers that she has been... sentenced to death!? Meanwhile, she becomes entangled in a drama between local government officials who could decide her fate!

The Apocalypse of Darkness Warfare

Publisher:
Future Digi co., Ltd.
Author:
WEI ZONG-CHENG

After the 1999 Jiji earthquake devastated Nantou, Taiwan, demons began to wreak havoc in the terrestrial realm, leading the police to set up ‘Project Blackout’ to eliminate them. One day, a terrible monster appears in Taipei. The police team up with the gifted teenage magician Chen Bo Jung to behead the beast and banish the evil spirit. In the process, Bo Jung runs into a girl who shares a name with the Goddess Matsu, worshipped by millions in the temples of Taiwan. Bo Jung takes the pretty Matsu under his wing only to discover that the unrest in the underworld and Matsu’s sudden appearance have a surprising hidden connection…

Invitation of Yama

Publisher:
Dyna Books Co., Ltd
Author:
YUZU

The underworld is where the dead should enter and where the living should not. Anyone holding the “Invitation of Yama” can enter the underworld freely. When Yama, the lord of the underworld, vanished without a trace, the duo Heibai Wuchang(Black and White Impermanence) also appeared in the world of the living. With the Invitation of Yama as a medium they bring people in and out of the underworld. During the search for Yama, they have come across various people and their stories of neglect, greed, and evil doings

Hades Items: Apocalypse

Publisher:
HALFTONE PRESS
Author:
SALAH-D

Wu-She Fan and Bi-An Xie are good friends for life, and remain tightly bonded even after death. Upon their death, the City God brings them under his control, and makes them the Seventh Lord and Eighth Lord, designated to guiding and guarding souls of the newly dead. However, an accident at a pilgrimage violates the principles of the Book of Life and Death, and reveals an unfulfilled wish Fan and Xie made over a century ago. Religious beliefs have their influence in the lives of people in Taiwan, but what do human beings actually believe in?

Oh, My Goddess!

Publisher:
Dala Publishing Company
Author:
CHIYOU(Amatiz)

Oh, My Goddess! brings Matsu and her festival to life with full-color depictions of the human and divine worlds during her birthday celebrations. Chiyou’s grand scenes, painted in deep, delicious color, recreate the festival in all its opulence and activity. Romanticized illustrations of Matsu and her divine pantheon might be especially attractive to younger readers, who can engage their imaginations as they learn more about this traditional Taiwanese festival.

My Girlfriend is a Ghost Cop

Publisher:
Frontier Anime Co., Ltd
Author:
JELLY WEI

Chang Yung, police captain of the underworld, saw ghosts when he was a child. One day, he broke the commandments and accidentally knocked his girlfriend unconscious, only to have her possessed by a male ghost! Criminal police officer Tsou Po An, murdered by gangsters in the line of duty, became a ghost possessing a woman’s body. The suspect he had been tailing before he died happened to be Chang Yung’s childhood friend A-jun, who lived across the street. To keep a close watch on the suspect, Bo An was reluctant to leave this body. At first, Chang Yung wanted to drive him out, but faced with a cop who was determined to complete the mission that he hadn’t lived to see through, Chang Yung decided to give up the idea, at least for the time being. Chang Yung couldn’t stand seeing Bo An running around like a headless chicken, and in order to protect his girlfriend’s body, he had no choice but to help the ghost cop with the investigation ….

Chio-Tian Young Beats

Publisher:
Dyna Books Co., Ltd
Author:
RUAN GUANG-MIN

The passionate shouts, the exhilarating drumbeats that ignite one's spirit, the sweat-drenched physical training, the daily practice of movements and actions – all of these are the essence of the CHIO-TIAN FOLK DRUMS & ARTS TROUPE. Hao Nan, a rebellious wanderer, temporarily resides in the CHIO-TIAN dormitory due to his beloved grandmother falling ill and being hospitalized. Listening to the wisdom and heartfelt stories of his senior peers from the martial arts community, Hao Nan witnesses the mutual encouragement among troupe members and feels the boundless warmth of their mentor. Will he, who once risked everything for justice as a youth, experience a transformation in his life? Renowned "popular" manga artist Ruan Guang Min portrays this generation-gap upbringing and the tumultuous journeys of young adults navigating the complexities of separated parents in a society reflected by real-life scenes.

“Why on earth … am I lying on my senior’s bed, half-naked and legs spread?” Lin Tzu Yun, a freshman at a top university, is determined to become a top student and Mr. Popularity in his department. To get over a heartbreaking relationship in high school and “join the ranks” successfully, he rents an apartment at a luxury condominium, only to discover that his roommate and landlord is a strikingly handsome senior. The senior is very nice and often treats Tzu Yun to food and drinks. He has long eyelashes, his body smells good, and he even says it’s okay to bring dates home. But why does he bring a different date home every day? And what does he mean by promising him a wonderful adult nightlife experience?! While Tzu Yun’s hopes of romantic encounters start to dwindle, living under the same roof as his senior seems to be getting more and more exciting …

Sleeping Brain

Publisher:
tōkhiu books
Author:
KUCHiXO

Kung Wei Hua was a second-generation Chinese immigrant. As an entomologist, his mission was to bring Taiwan’s endemic butterfly species back to the motherland. When he was looking for the Papilio maraho (nicknamed “Butterfly of Dreams”) in the forests of Taiping Mountain, he met Kiichi Aramura, a Japanese amateur photographer capturing the beauty of butterflies with his camera. The two started conversing from time to time and, as if by fate, encountered a faceless, mute butterfly catcher wandering deep in the woods. In a series of strange events, they discovered the truth behind the butterfly catcher’s lost voice—the horrible experience during the White Terror. But did all of this really happen in this dense forest? Or was there no one around to hear it?
